Khmer Rouge is Cambodia's nightmare

The Khmer Rouge regime, led by Pol Pot, ruled Cambodia from 1975 to 1979. During that time, one in five Cambodians died from overwork, starvation or homicide. Here you can research and learn more about the tragedy of the Cambodian nation through images, audio and video. Listen to the stories of those who went through the Khmer Rouge regime, see the change of country, and share family stories Those who have gone through this regime.
